Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 26, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how CliffCircuit ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our AI assistant services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Phone Number: Provided voluntarily for SMS notifications and verification codes.
  • Communication Data: Messages and commands sent to the AI assistant.
  • System Logs: Technical data necessary for service operation.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Sending SMS notifications and verification codes you request
  • Responding to your queries and commands
  • Improving the AI assistant's functionality
  • Maintaining service security and reliability

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your data is only shared with:

  • Service providers (e.g., Twilio for SMS delivery) necessary to operate the service
  • When required by law or to protect rights and safety

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including encryption and secure storage on macOS Keychain.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of SMS messages by texting STOP

7. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: cliffcircuit@proton.me
