Independent Sales Representative Agreement

Mavyn — Premium Peptide Distribution
Effective Date: ___________________

1. Parties

This Agreement is entered into between Mavyn ("Company") and the undersigned individual ("Representative"). By signing below, Representative agrees to all terms set forth herein.

2. Appointment & Scope

Company appoints Representative as a non-exclusive independent sales representative. Representative will promote and sell Mavyn products to personal contacts and within their territory using word-of-mouth and relationship-based sales. Representative is an independent contractor — not an employee, agent, partner, or franchisee of Mavyn.

3. Mavyn Pro Platform Access

One-Time Platform Fee: $199.00
Access to the Mavyn Pro platform is required to place orders, track commissions, and manage client accounts. This fee is payable to Mavyn upon signing and is non-refundable.

The Mavyn Pro platform provides Representatives with:

  • Access to the full product catalog with live pricing
  • Order submission and tracking tools
  • Commission dashboard and payment history
  • Training materials and sales resources

6. Recruiting & Team Override

Representatives may recruit new Representatives into the Mavyn network. When a Representative recruits a new active Representative:

  • The recruiting Representative earns a 5% lifetime override on all sales made by their recruit, for as long as both parties remain active.
  • The override is paid by Mavyn — it does not reduce the recruit's commission.
  • A $199 Mavyn Pro platform fee is collected from each new recruit at onboarding. This fee goes to Mavyn in full.
  • Overrides are limited to one level — recruits of recruits do not generate additional override income.
  • If a recruit's monthly sales fall below 00 for two consecutive months, the override is paused until the recruit resumes active status.

7. Pricing & Sales Terms

  • All products must be sold at Mavyn's published retail prices. Undercutting is prohibited.
  • Representative may not offer unauthorized discounts, promotions, or custom pricing without written Company approval.
  • All orders must be placed through the Mavyn Pro platform.

8. Client Ownership

Clients introduced to Mavyn by Representative are considered Mavyn clients. Representative retains preferential credit for those clients' orders while this Agreement is active. Upon termination, client relationships revert entirely to Mavyn.

9. Supplier Confidentiality

Representative acknowledges that Mavyn's supplier relationships, pricing structures, and sourcing information are strictly confidential. Representative may not contact, engage, or transact with Mavyn's suppliers directly — now or at any time after this Agreement ends. Violation of this provision will result in immediate termination and may result in legal action.

10. Term & Termination

  • This Agreement is effective upon signing and continues month-to-month.
  • Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice.
  • Mavyn may terminate immediately for cause, including: fraud, misrepresentation, supplier contact violations, pricing violations, or conduct damaging to Mavyn's reputation.
  • Upon termination, all access to the Mavyn Pro platform ceases. Earned commissions through the termination date will be paid on schedule. No future overrides will accrue.

11. Independent Contractor Status

Representative is solely responsible for all federal, state, and local taxes on income earned under this Agreement. Mavyn will not withhold taxes or provide benefits of any kind. Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment relationship.

12. Product Claims & Compliance

Representative agrees not to make medical claims, dosing recommendations, or health guarantees about any Mavyn product. All sales must be presented as research compounds only. Representative assumes full responsibility for compliance with applicable laws in their territory.

13. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all prior agreements or understandings. Amendments require written consent from both parties.
